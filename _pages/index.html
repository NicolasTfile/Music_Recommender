About Us

The inspiration for this project came mainly from our love for music. There's nothing quite like discovering a new song that instantly becomes a favorite. Music recommendations can come from various sources, but many music apps are known for compromising user data and prioritizing ads over music discovery.

With Music Recommender, we set out to create a user-centric platform that rekindles the joy of finding amazing music. Our project promises an immersive experience, putting the user's musical journey first.

This project was developed as part of Holberton School's portfolio projects. Feel free to connect with our team members on LinkedIn, explore our GitHub repository, and follow us on Twitter.
